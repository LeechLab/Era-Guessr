EXPLORE THE PAST

Guess the year presented by the historical photographs. Compete against other players to climb the leaderboards! Earn legenderay prizes by unlocking achievements!

HOW IT WORKS:

First, Era Guessr grabs 5 random images each year themed to the gamemode (you can create your own custom game if you want to!). Then, it's your job to guess the year each media represents! Guess each year accurately to get the best score!
The faster and more accurate your guesses are, the higher you climb the public Leaderboards! You can also equip legendary outfits (historically-themed of course)! Unlock achivements to change how your Guessr looks!

Era Guessr is created for enjoying history through images.
All of these images are not owned or affiliated by us in anyway. These images were gathered from the internet and stored onto a database.
